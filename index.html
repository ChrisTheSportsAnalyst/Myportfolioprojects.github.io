Bicycle Sales

Bicycles have become a popular mode of transportation and leisure activity, and the bike industry has seen a steady increase in sales over the years. However, understanding the factors that influence someone's decision to purchase a bike is crucial for bike manufacturers and retailers to target the right audience and improve sales. In this project, we will analyze data on bike sales and customer demographics to answer the following questions: Who is more likely to purchase a bike? What factors affect someone's decision to purchase a bike? Who purchases bicycles more?
